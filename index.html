Post-Labor Economics Governance Process Standard
Institutions for a World After Work
A complete guide to designing, operating, and evolving post-labor institutions using the PLE Governance Process Standard
This book provides the first comprehensive process standard for solidarity economy institutions — drawing on the APQC Process Classification Framework and adapting it for the community benefit organizations, worker cooperatives, community land trusts, mutual aid networks, and civic technology cooperatives that will constitute the institutional backbone of post-labor economies. Sixteen GPS categories, eighty process groups, and approximately 1,400 institutional activities are organized across three tiers and assessed using a four-level maturity model, with a full KPI library, maturity assessment tool, recommended resources, and glossary.
